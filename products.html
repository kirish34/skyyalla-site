TekeTeke | Public transport

TekeTeke - wallets for matatus, buses, taxis and bodas

Our first product going live. Built around SACCOs, stages and the reality of targets, daily fees and crew rotations.

With TekeTeke, each vehicle gets its own wallet. Fares come in, SACCO fees, savings and loan deductions are applied, and what remains is the closing balance. Everyone - owner, crew and SACCO - can see the same numbers.

  • Wallet per vehicle and per SACCO
  • Daily fee, savings and loan deduction rules
  • USSD and Paybill flows for passengers
  • Dashboards for SACCO staff, management and owners

FlashPay | Retail and supermarkets

FlashPay - USSD checkout for shops and supermarkets

Short USSD codes and STK push at the till. Designed to reduce queues and manual errors, and to give owners clean branch-level reporting.

With FlashPay, a customer dials a short USSD code linked to a branch or till, approves the M-Pesa STK push, and the cashier is done. It can sit on top of a full POS system or work with a basic phone at the counter.

  • USSD codes mapped to branches, tills and cashiers
  • Automatic STK push on top of M-Pesa
  • Success or failure feedback to your system (where integrated)
  • Branch and cashier-level analytics for owners

Capital Assets | Rent and property

Capital Assets - rent collections with clear trails

Wallets per unit, per building and per landlord. Every rent payment is tied to the right house and month, automatically.

Capital Assets gives landlords and property managers a clear view of who has paid, who is late, and how each building is performing. Tenants pay via USSD or Paybill, and payments are mapped to the correct unit and period.

  • Wallets per building, per unit and per landlord account
  • Arrears tracking and payment history per tenant
  • Exports for accountants and owners
